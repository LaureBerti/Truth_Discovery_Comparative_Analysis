Details

Both Ts initialization value as well as λ are used in the 2-Estimates and 3-Estimates algorithms. The next results are for the 3-Estimates algorithm.
  • We started by varying source trustworthiness initialization: Ts for fixed ε=0.1 and fixed λ=0.1.
    Ts=0.8 achieved best precision of 0.9805.

  • Then, we fixed Ts=0.8 and ε=0.1, and, varying λ
    3-Estimates achieved high precision for λ=0.8, but this precision was unstable over several runs, it varied from 0.9756 to 0.9934. Thus we did not choose λ=0.8. Meanwhile, λ=0.5 achieved high precision with stable performance over several runs.

  • Fixing Ts=0.8 and λ=0.5, we varied ε.
    Finally, we picked ε=0.4 for maximal precision.
